# Company Handbook - Rules of Engagement

## Core Principles

1. **Local-First**: All data stays local in this Obsidian vault
2. **Privacy-Focused**: Secrets (tokens, banking creds) never sync to cloud
3. **Human-in-the-Loop**: Sensitive actions require manual approval
4. **Transparent**: All actions logged in Markdown files

## Communication Rules

### Email Handling
- Always be professional and polite
- Flag urgent emails (keywords: "urgent", "asap", "deadline")
- Draft replies for review before sending (Silver tier+)

### WhatsApp Handling
- Respond within 24 hours for non-urgent messages
- Immediate response for keywords: "urgent", "asap", "emergency"
- Always confirm receipt of important requests

## Financial Rules

### Payment Thresholds
| Amount | Action Required |
|--------|-----------------|
| < $100 | Auto-categorize only |
| $100 - $500 | Flag for review |
| > $500 | Require explicit approval |

### Subscription Monitoring
- Flag any recurring charge not previously authorized
- Alert if cost increases > 20%
- Report unused subscriptions (no activity in 30 days)

## Task Processing Rules

1. **Check /Needs_Action** folder first
2. **Create Plan.md** for multi-step tasks
3. **Move to /Done** when complete
4. **Log all actions** in relevant files

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to human when:
- Payment > $500 detected
- Legal/contract language in messages
- Ambiguous requests requiring clarification
- Any error that cannot be recovered

## Working Hours

- **Monitoring**: 24/7 (when scripts are running)
- **Response Time**: Within 1 hour during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM)
- **After Hours**: Queue for next business day
